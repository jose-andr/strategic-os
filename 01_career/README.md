# Career

## Purpose

This folder contains career development assets that support long-term strategic positioning, promotion readiness and future opportunities.

Use this folder to capture evidence of value, feedback, achievements, capability development and career direction.

## What belongs here

- Achievements
- Feedback
- Career goals
- Promotion readiness notes
- Opportunity reviews
- Career guidance outputs
- Role or position analysis
- Capability gap assessments
- Career narratives
- Evidence of strategic influence
- Evidence of organisational impact
- Evidence of customer-centred practice
- Evidence of systems thinking
- Evidence of AI-enabled capability

## What does not belong here

- Raw organisational source data
- Confidential HR records
- Sensitive stakeholder commentary
- Customer-level records
- Full project documentation
- Governed datasets
- Private organisational documents

Use summaries, proof points and reusable career evidence instead.

## Suggested structure

    01_career/
    ├── achievements/
    ├── feedback/
    ├── opportunities/
    └── promotion_readiness/

## Subfolder purposes

### `achievements/`

Use for evidence of completed work, impact, contribution and outcomes.

### `feedback/`

Use for feedback received from managers, peers, stakeholders or collaborators.

### `opportunities/`

Use for career opportunities, role options, stretch work or strategic positioning opportunities.

### `promotion_readiness/`

Use for promotion evidence, readiness assessments, role criteria mapping and progression narratives.

## Career evidence flow

Use this flow to convert work into career value:

    project artefact
    → achievement note
    → promotion proof point
    → career narrative
    → role application or development conversation

## Evidence categories

Career evidence should be organised around:

- strategic influence
- organisational impact
- customer-centred practice
- systems thinking
- human-centred design
- AI-enabled capability
- stakeholder influence
- delivery and follow-through

## Suggested naming

Use date-first filenames.

Examples:

- `2026-06-15_achievement_service-account-eofy-story.md`
- `2026-06-15_feedback_stakeholder-alignment.md`
- `2026-06-15_promotion-readiness_business-design-advisor.md`
- `2026-06-15_career-guidance_strategic-positioning.md`

## Review rhythm

Review this folder monthly.

Use the review to ask:

- What evidence has been created?
- What opportunities are emerging?
- What capability gaps remain?
- What should become a promotion proof point?
- What should become a reusable career story?

## Review checklist

Before saving career material, check:

- [ ] Is the material safe to retain?
- [ ] Is sensitive information removed or generalised?
- [ ] Is this evidence, interpretation or aspiration?
- [ ] Does it support long-term strategic positioning?
- [ ] Could it support promotion readiness?
- [ ] Does it connect to the desired reputation?
- [ ] Is there a clear next action?
