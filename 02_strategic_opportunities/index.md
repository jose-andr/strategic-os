# Strategic Opportunities Index

## Purpose

This index tracks strategic opportunities that may support the Strategic OS North Star:

> Increase strategic opportunities by helping leaders make better strategic decisions.

Use this file to make opportunities visible, reviewable and actionable over time.

It is not a task list.

It is a lightweight pipeline for opportunities that may become projects, relationships, career evidence, thought leadership, decisions or future work.

## Opportunity States

Use these states to track movement.

| State | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Captured | Noted as a possible opportunity, but not yet assessed |
| Shaping | Being clarified, framed or developed |
| Socialising | Being discussed with relevant people |
| Active | Currently being pursued |
| Parked | Useful, but not right for now |
| Declined | Intentionally not pursued |
| Converted to project | Moved into `08_projects/` |
| Converted to career evidence | Moved into `01_career/` |
| Converted to thought leadership | Moved into `09_thought_leadership/` |

## Assessment Fields

### Date Captured

Use the date the opportunity was first identified.

Use ISO format:

`YYYY-MM-DD`

### Opportunity

Name the opportunity clearly and safely.

Avoid confidential project names, sensitive stakeholder names or internal details that should not be stored in the repo.

### State

Use one of the standard opportunity states.

### Strategic Value

Summarise why the opportunity may matter strategically.

Consider whether it could:

- improve decision quality
- strengthen stakeholder alignment
- create organisational value
- improve customer outcomes
- demonstrate systems thinking
- build reusable capability
- support the desired reputation

### Stakeholder Value

Summarise who may benefit and why.

Keep this privacy-safe and abstract where needed.

### Next Action

Name the next useful step.

Examples:

- clarify the opportunity
- gather evidence
- draft opportunity review
- discuss with sponsor
- connect to active project
- park until timing improves
- convert to project folder
- convert to career evidence
- convert to thought leadership note

### Related File

Link or name the related file when one exists.

Useful destinations may include:

- `10_templates/opportunity_review.md`
- `04_frameworks/strategic_opportunity_pipeline.md`
- `08_projects/`
- `01_career/`
- `09_thought_leadership/`
- `03_decision_briefs/`

## Review Questions

Use these questions during monthly opportunity review.

### Strategic Fit

- Does this opportunity support the Strategic OS North Star?
- Could it help leaders make better strategic decisions?
- Does it align with the desired reputation?
- Is it connected to real organisational or customer value?

### Evidence

- What evidence supports this opportunity?
- What is still assumed?
- What is uncertain?
- What would make this opportunity stronger?

### Timing

- Is now the right time?
- Is there a decision window?
- Is there stakeholder readiness?
- Is there organisational momentum?
- Should this be active, shaped or parked?

### Stakeholders

- Who needs to understand this opportunity?
- Who could support it?
- Who may be affected by it?
- What alignment is needed before moving forward?

### Conversion

- Should this become a project?
- Should this become career evidence?
- Should this become a thought leadership piece?
- Should this become a decision brief?
- Should this be declined or parked?

## Monthly Review Pattern

During monthly review:

1. Add new captured opportunities.
2. Review active and shaping opportunities.
3. Move opportunities between states.
4. Convert mature opportunities into projects, evidence or thought leadership.
5. Park or decline low-value opportunities.
6. Remove stale items that no longer matter.

## Privacy Guidance

Do not include:

- confidential stakeholder names
- customer-level details
- sensitive organisational issues
- private employee matters
- raw internal source extracts
- governed dataset details
- unresolved sensitive risks

Use safe abstractions.

Instead of:

`Jane Smith wants me to lead the confidential complaints redesign`

Use:

`possible service redesign leadership opportunity`

Instead of:

`customer complaint case 12345 shows failure in vulnerable customer handling`

Use:

`vulnerable customer service improvement opportunity`

## Success Measures

This index is working when:

- opportunities are visible
- promising ideas are not lost
- timing and next actions are clear
- low-value opportunities are intentionally parked or declined
- active opportunities connect to projects, evidence or decisions
- career evidence becomes easier to assemble
- strategic opportunity review becomes easier each month

## Failure Modes

This index is failing if:

- it becomes a dumping ground
- every idea is treated as an opportunity
- states are not updated
- opportunities have no next action
- sensitive details are stored unnecessarily
- old opportunities remain active without review
- the index becomes disconnected from projects, career evidence or decisions

## Maintenance

Review this index monthly.

During quarterly repository hygiene review:

- remove stale opportunities
- archive declined opportunities if useful
- convert mature opportunities to the right folder
- check that active opportunities still support the North Star
- ensure sensitive details are not exposed
