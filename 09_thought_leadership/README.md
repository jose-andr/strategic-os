# Thought Leadership

## Purpose

This folder contains reusable ideas, reflections, drafts and public-facing thinking that may support professional visibility, career positioning and strategic influence.

Use this folder to develop ideas that translate Strategic OS learning into shareable perspectives on business design, human-centred design, systems thinking, customer experience, service design, AI-enabled ways of working and strategic decision-making.

## What belongs here

- Draft articles
- Reflection notes
- Speaking ideas
- Presentation concepts
- LinkedIn post drafts
- Reusable points of view
- Professional narrative drafts
- Thought pieces based on lessons learned
- Safe summaries of strategic patterns
- Ideas that support long-term positioning

## What does not belong here

- Confidential organisational information
- Raw organisational source data
- Customer-level records
- Sensitive stakeholder commentary
- Governed datasets
- Private organisational records
- Internal-only project details
- Claims that are not supported by evidence or experience

Use generalised, safe and portable insights instead.

## Thought leadership themes

Potential themes include:

- strategic decision-making
- business design
- human-centred design
- customer-centred organisations
- service systems
- stakeholder alignment
- AI-enabled strategy work
- sensemaking and ambiguity
- translating evidence into action
- operating models and service design
- career development through strategic influence

## Review rhythm

Review this folder monthly.

Use the review to ask:

- Which ideas are worth developing?
- Which drafts are ready to socialise?
- Which ideas support the desired reputation?
- Which drafts need stronger evidence or examples?
- Which topics are too sensitive to share?
- Which ideas should become frameworks instead?

## Review checklist

Before saving or sharing thought leadership, check:

- [ ] Is the idea clear and useful?
- [ ] Is the content safe to retain or share?
- [ ] Is organisational detail removed or generalised?
- [ ] Are claims supported by evidence, experience or reasoning?
- [ ] Does it support long-term strategic positioning?
- [ ] Does it strengthen the desired reputation?
- [ ] Is the next action clear?
