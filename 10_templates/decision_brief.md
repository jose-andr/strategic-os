# Decision Brief

## Purpose

This template turns ambiguity, evidence, options and trade-offs into a decision-ready recommendation.

It is designed to help the user and decision-makers understand:

* what decision is required
* why the decision matters
* what evidence is available
* what options were considered
* what trade-offs exist
* what is recommended
* what action should happen next

This template is primarily supported by the Sensemaking Agent.

North Star:

> Increase strategic opportunities by helping leaders make better strategic decisions.

## When To Use

Use this template when a decision needs to be clarified, prepared, recommended or escalated.

Use it for:

* strategic choices
* project direction
* prioritisation
* investment or effort trade-offs
* stakeholder alignment decisions
* service improvement options
* operating model changes
* opportunity assessment
* risk acceptance
* shipping or stopping decisions that need formal framing

Do not use this template for:

* raw notes
* simple task lists
* temporary admin
* meeting minutes
* unresolved thinking with no decision point
* sensitive source material that should not be stored

## Inputs

Useful inputs may include:

* raw notes
* project context
* stakeholder input
* customer insight
* operational evidence
* data summaries
* options analysis
* risk notes
* workshop outputs
* service or system maps
* previous decisions
* unresolved questions

## Review Checklist

Before using this decision brief, check:

* Is the decision required clear?
* Is the recommendation clear?
* Is evidence separated from assumptions?
* Are trade-offs visible?
* Are options proportionate?
* Are risks and mitigations clear?
* Are stakeholder implications handled carefully?
* Is sensitive information removed or generalised?
* Is human review required before use?
* Is the next action specific?
* Does the brief support better strategic decision-making?

## Output Quality Bar

A useful decision brief should be:

* concise enough to read
* specific enough to decide from
* honest about uncertainty
* clear about trade-offs
* grounded in evidence
* practical about implementation
* respectful of stakeholder context
* explicit about human review
* safe to store or share where appropriate

## Failure Modes

This template is not working if:

* it becomes a long report
* the decision required is unclear
* options are listed without trade-offs
* weak evidence is overstated
* assumptions are hidden
* the recommendation is vague
* stakeholder impact is ignored
* risks are minimised
* implementation is unrealistic
* sensitive source material is copied into the brief
