# Shipping Recommendation

## Purpose

Use this template to decide whether a piece of work should be shipped, socialised, refined or stopped.

This template supports the Shipping Coach.

## When to use

Use this when:

- A draft is sitting unfinished
- Work is becoming over-refined
- It is unclear whether something is ready to share
- Momentum is slowing
- More feedback is needed before further refinement
- A piece of work may no longer be worth continuing
