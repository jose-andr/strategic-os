# Stakeholder Alignment Assessment

## Purpose

Use this template to assess who needs to understand, support, shape or act on a piece of work for it to succeed.

This template supports the Stakeholder Journey Agent.

## When to use

Use this when:

- A recommendation needs stakeholder support
- A decision is not progressing
- There are competing perspectives
- A project needs clearer engagement sequencing
- Resistance, ambiguity or silence may affect progress
- A message needs to be adapted for different audiences
