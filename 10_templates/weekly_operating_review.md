# Weekly Operating Review

## Purpose

This template helps the Chief of Staff Agent review the week, close open loops and prepare the next operating cycle.

It is designed to help the user:

* review what moved forward
* identify decisions made or still required
* close or carry forward follow-ups
* surface risks and blockers
* protect focus for the next week
* connect weekly activity to the Strategic OS North Star

North Star:

> Increase strategic opportunities by helping leaders make better strategic decisions.

## When To Use

Use this template at the end of each week or before planning the next week.

Use it when there are:

* multiple active projects
* unresolved follow-ups
* decisions waiting for action
* stakeholder conversations to track
* risks or blockers that need escalation
* opportunities emerging from the week
* lessons worth capturing
* work that needs to be shipped, socialised, refined or stopped

## Inputs

Useful inputs may include:

* daily briefings
* calendar notes
* meeting notes
* project updates
* follow-up lists
* inbox items
* decision notes
* stakeholder concerns
* shipping recommendations

* strategic opportunity notes

## Review Checklist

Before using the weekly review, check:

* Is the week’s progress clear?
* Are unresolved decisions visible?
* Are follow-ups specific?
* Are risks and blockers named?
* Are stakeholder assumptions marked?
* Are strategic opportunity signals captured?
* Are lessons only stored when reusable?
* Is career evidence grounded in real work?
* Is sensitive information removed or generalised?
* Is next week’s focus realistic?
* Are first actions specific and useful?
* Does the review support the Strategic OS North Star?

## Output Quality Bar

A useful weekly operating review should be:

* concise enough to use
* honest about what moved and what stalled
* clear about decisions required
* practical about follow-ups
* sensitive to stakeholders
* realistic about capacity
* useful for next-week planning
* safe to store
* connected to strategic opportunities and better decisions

## Failure Modes

This template is not working if:

* it becomes a diary rather than an operating review
* it lists activity without judging progress
* it hides stalled work
* it treats every follow-up as equally important
* it stores sensitive details unnecessarily
* it creates more work than clarity
* it misses decision-required items
* it ignores stakeholder movement
* it fails to identify what should be shipped, socialised, refined or stopped
* it does not make next week easier to start
