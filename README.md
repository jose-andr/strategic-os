# Strategic OS

## Purpose

Strategic OS is a personal augmentation system designed to help transform complexity into strategic decisions, improve stakeholder alignment, and create more strategic opportunities.

The system supports a Business Design and Human-Centred Design practice through AI-enabled sensemaking, decision support, stakeholder management and career development.

---

## North Star

Increase strategic opportunities by helping leaders make better strategic decisions.

---

## Desired Reputation

> "He helps us make better strategic decisions."

---

## Strategic Positioning

A customer-centred strategic advisor who connects:

- Customer outcomes
- Organisational outcomes
- Evidence and insights
- Systems thinking
- AI-enabled ways of working

---

## Why This Exists

My strongest value is not producing artefacts.

My value comes from:

- Framing the right problem
- Making sense of complexity
- Connecting customer and organisational outcomes
- Developing practical recommendations
- Supporting strategic decision-making

This system exists to amplify those strengths while reducing common constraints such as:

- Perfectionism
- Over-analysis
- Starting too many initiatives
- Delayed stakeholder engagement
- Difficulty translating insights into organisational action

---

## Design Principles

### Decision First

Outputs should be structured around decisions rather than findings.

### Human In The Loop

Humans make decisions.

Agents provide synthesis, recommendations, prompts and coaching.

### Privacy By Design

No organisational source data is stored in this repository.

Only personal knowledge, frameworks, observations and reusable patterns are retained.

### Employer Independent

Knowledge should remain portable and useful throughout a career.

### Markdown First

All strategic knowledge should be AI-readable and future-proof.

### Ship Over Perfect

Progress, learning and influence are prioritised over unnecessary refinement.
